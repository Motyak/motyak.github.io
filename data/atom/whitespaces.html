12 8 <> 128
0 x 10 <> 0x10
in line <> inline
as sign <> assign
count-1 <> count - 1
buffer-size <> buffer - size
foreach(x) <> foreach (x)

---

- Whitespaces play a huge role in affecting
  a meaning to a sentence (for both humans
  and compilers):
    -> 12 8 <> 128
    -> in line <> inline
    -> as sign <> assign

- what looks like a symbol should be a symbol,
  what looks like an operation should be an operation,
  and what looks like a number should be a number :
    -> buffer-size <> buffer - size
    -> 0 x 10 <> 0x10

- what looks like a function should be a function,
  and what looks like a statement should be a statement :
    -> foreach(x) <> foreach (x)
